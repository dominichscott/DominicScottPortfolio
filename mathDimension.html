MATH DIMENSION

Developed by myself and Danny Murray, Math Dimension is a Mathematic Puzzle game disguised as Edutainment, where randomly generated operators are created. Play as a crocodile named Al (short for Alligator) and help him decipher every indecipherable operator: from Cromp to Yulb to Addition!

This project was developed in Unity 3D and C# for a class, featuring a dialogue system, first person movement, a persistent notebook between levels, and quizes to put players to the test.
